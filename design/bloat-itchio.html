// what is this

Your score is
your enemy.

BLOAT is an arcade survival game where every point you collect grows a physical body attached to you. Not a number. Not a bar. A real chain of segments that trails behind your every move.

The body widens your turning radius. It draws enemy fire. It blocks your own escape routes. The better you play, the worse your situation becomes.

At any moment you can shed — release half your body to buy speed. The discarded segments harden into obstacles for 3 seconds before dissolving. You escape one problem and create another.

01
Collect
Pickups always spawn in dangerous positions. Never free. Never safe. Never optional if you want to survive long enough to matter.
02
Bloat
Each pickup adds one segment. Your turn radius degrades. At 30+ segments you're planning 3 seconds ahead — or dying.
03
Multiply
Score multiplier = current segment count. 40 segments means 40× points per pickup. Risk and reward are the same thing.
04
Shed
Sacrifice the back half. Regain your turn. But the dropped segments become walls. You just rebuilt the maze around yourself.
